Pricing Philosophy

Pay for analysts, not your whole team.

Most CI tools charge per user. A 200-person company with one PMM running CI would pay $40K+/year at Klue to give sales reps access to battle cards.

DOSI.AI charges for analyst seats — the people doing the CI work — and lets your entire org consume the output as free Viewer seats.

A 200-person company with 1 PMM running CI pays $279/month for the PMM seat. The 199 other people consume battle cards, briefs, and the feed for free. That's the difference between a $40K/year tool and a $3,348/year tool.
